About Me

I’ve carried countless hobbies and curiosities through the years—drifting in and out like passing seasons. No clear pattern, no tidy category. But behind the ever-changing interests, there’s always been one constant thread: a quiet obsession with the human spirit and how it reveals itself.
This space is born from that fixation. Here, you’ll stumble upon strange art, scattered thoughts, fragments of hope, shadowed reflections, and the occasional descent into beautiful madness. It’s not linear, and it’s not meant to be.
I’m captivated by people—their lives, their marks, their chaos and creation. If you find yourself drawn to the same, then you’re already home. Welcome to my realm of Musings and Media. <3
